An investigation of the University of Waterloo effect

An investigation of the University of Waterloo effect


Students want to get their STEM education from a well qualified university. These universities can be Stevens Institute of Technology, New Jersey Institute of Technology, but there can also come the University of Waterloo. Waterloo is known for its rigorous academics, challenging students in the most unfathomable of ways. However, on social media, STEM students have recently been showing the hilarious yet concerning effects the University of Waterloo has on them.

One change Waterloo is known for enacting on its students is baldness. Various students on TikTok show a before and after picture of themselves. The most noticeable change mentioned by comments has been baldness, which is caused by insane amounts of stress, demonstrating Waterloo’s intense workload. The “after” pictures of many people have also included a sunken, drained expression, conveying that Waterloo’s rigor has extremely affected their wellbeing. Although this may seem sad, it’s okay to laugh, since students find humor in this crazy change. Here’s a youtube shorts showing the dramatic before-after pictures of a guy, although he is not balding:





In fact, on the bright side, not all students have faced the detrimental Waterloo effects. It has been an ongoing joke that females are “immune” to the Waterloo effects, since their before and after pictures stay virtually the same, with barely any change. While girl power exists in this aspect, females are not the only immune students. There have been some males who have also somehow passed through rigorous, competitive years at the University of Waterloo. However, people are in disbelief of this possibility, claiming it is “engineering/Waterloo propaganda.” That is how impressive it is for the immune students to be so immune to the Waterloo effects.



This university is certainly a unique one, containing a very strong standard on academic excellence and teaching its students the concepts in such a way so that they will never forget them. While the university of Waterloo is sometimes too harsh, it is also insanely rewarding especially to the immune students.
