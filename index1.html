SHEIN & Temu about to increase in price, consumers are upset


With increasing trade war tensions and tariffs between the US and China, it is without a doubt that the prices of imported items from China – especially items sold on SHEIN and Temu – will increase.

For those who are not familiar with these apps, SHEIN and Temu are best known for selling cheap and practical items, kind of like a 5 below but online. However, there has recently been a trade war between the US and China, as said before. For context, US President Trump had thrown a total of about 104% tariffs on China as of April 9, 2025, meaning any imported items from China will be more expensive to ship to the US. Now, you may be asking something along the lines of, “what does that have to do with me? It’s just a country-to-country tariff competition.” Well, yes, it may seem like a competition due to China retaliating to these tariffs as well, but you, the consumer, are most definitely affected by it. These tariffs usually hit companies and businesses’ shipping areas, but they do not want to pay for that, so they will pass these tariffs onto you by increasing the price, basically incorporating that tariff inside of the items they sell to you. This is exactly what happens in every business, including but not limited to SHEIN and Temu. Sure, they had been cheaper before, but now they will not be the same.

Many SHEIN and Temu buyers are upset about the potential price increase, especially those who are living in financial crises . To them, these apps were deemed to be budget friendly options, providing cheap clothing/jewelry/gadgets with decent quality. In response to the increases in price, user @plobthebakerman’s video on TikTok conveys the upset mood she faces upon realising the 104% tariff applying to SHEIN and Temu and includes her regretting not buying items from her cart soon enough. Fortunately, there has been increasing awareness on this issue throughout TikTok and other social media. Many people also started warning others to stop shopping from SHEIN and Temu completely in spite of the price increases and in protest of lowering the prices.


The future of SHEIN and Temu and their price changes now remain unknown. Since they are increasing, there will be reduced spending due to consumer boycotts. However, it is not known if the increasing prices will ever go back down. To avoid paying the heavier price, SHEIN and Temu customers will most likely now plan on shopping elsewhere.
