What’s going on with Justin & Hailey Bieber?


Justin and Hailey Bieber seem like such a nice couple who have it all. However, lately, their marriage issues have been talked about and exposed to the public eye. For those who aren’t familiar with this situation, buckle up for a deep dive into this story.



This situation dates all the way back to 2016. During that year, Justin Bieber and Hailey Baldwin split up from their relationship and went their separate ways. A year later, Justin Bieber went to date Selena Gomez for a while up until 2018. However, this relationship did not work out, and so Justin went back to date Hailey again. In September of 2018, Justin and Hailey both got married and eventually had a child in 2022. Now, you see this relationship is going very well. However, drama unfolds once again starting January 21, 2025, where Justin was questioned by his followers about supposedly unfollowing Hailey on Instagram and Hailey unfollowing Justin. Later, Justin curbed the rumors and questions by claiming his account was hacked and caused the unfollowing. Hailey also claimed that her unfollowing Justin was a glitch, not intentional. Eventually, everybody got the message, and since then, no Bieber posts provoked any rumors.



As you can see, the Bieber relationship at first had been quite tenuous, but later on worked out. There are still rumors going around, though, about the Biebers talking about a possible divorce. To try and curb those rumors, the Biebers regularly post content suggesting their relationship is well off despite fans’ speculations regarding Justin’s mental health by observing his changing fashion style, which has also been debunked because Justin had indirectly clarified plenty of times that his change in style was to hint to his new upcoming fashion line, SKYLRK.




Although there are rumors going around talking about the Biebers possibly divorcing, the Biebers seem to be committed and happy in their relationship. There is no intense drama going around with the Biebers, especially through their constant clarifications and happy posts. There is no need to be thinking about them divorcing.
